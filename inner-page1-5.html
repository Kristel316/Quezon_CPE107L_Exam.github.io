  2. SECURITY SERVICES OFFICE

The Security Services Office (SSO) prioritizes the safety of its stakeholders and therefore dedicates itself to a well-crafted safety and security plan backed by operational state-of-the-art systems.
Every building and floor has stationed security to ensure safety and communication all across campus. Aside from state-of-the-art systems, MCM boasts its team of trained security personnel, especially in times of emergency and disaster risk management.


SERVICES

  • SAFETY AND SECURITY
    1. To ensure the safety of the MCM community, the SSO strictly implements standard security protocols on campus, exercising maximum tolerance towards both insiders and outsiders committing wrongful acts or misdemeanors.
    2. Regular ID verification.
    3. Bag inspection upon entry into campus to ensure no bringing of prohibited items.
    4. 24 hour patrolling of campus with guard stations on each floor and building.
    5. Strict prevention of any unauthorized persons attempting to enter campus grounds.
    6. Swift communication between personnel for official business purposes and emergencies.


  • EXECUTION OF EMERGENCY PLAN
    1. Quick and initial response to emergency situations.
    2. Ensures student, employee and guest safety during emergency situations.
